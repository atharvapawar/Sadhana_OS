# Phase 5 — 18 Stage Intelligence Cards

This document records the stage-card standard used by Sadhana OS. The live implementation is in `src/data/stage-intelligence.ts` and is rendered inside the existing stage detail route.

## Purpose

Each stage should feel like part of one river, not a disconnected article. The repeated structure gives every stage the same sequence of understanding:

1. Essence
2. Why this stage comes here
3. What it purifies or stabilizes
4. Connected concepts
5. Beginner-safe practice
6. Common mistake
7. Teacher-guided depth
8. How it supports the next stage

## Content Rules

- Keep the 18 stages and 5 arcs unchanged.
- Do not turn stage pages into encyclopedias.
- Keep advanced practices visible as traditional knowledge, but safety-gated.
- Always separate beginner-safe action from teacher-guided or teacher-only depth.
- Every stage must show how it supports the next stage.

## Safety Rule

The intelligence cards may describe advanced practices as existing within the tradition, but they must not provide initiation-like instructions, forced breath retention, unsafe cleansing directions, or kundalini activation methods.
