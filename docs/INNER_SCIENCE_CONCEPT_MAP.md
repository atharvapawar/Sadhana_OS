# Inner Science Concept Map

Status: Phase 6 implemented.

Purpose: connect the traditional maps of the human instrument without creating overlap confusion, unsafe practice instructions, or scattered knowledge.

## North Star

The Yogic River from Misidentification to Living Awareness.

Inner Science explains the instrument through which misidentification appears and through which practice matures:

- Sharira Traya: gross, subtle, causal body language
- Pancha Kosha: food, prana, mind, wisdom, bliss sheaths
- Antahkarana: manas, chitta, ahamkara, buddhi, and the witness distinction
- Prana, Vayu, and Nadi: life-current and subtle-channel language
- Tattva, Guna, and Dosha: manifestation, quality, and constitution
- Chakra and Granthi language: subtle center and knot language

## Content Rules

1. Inner Science is explanatory, not procedural.
2. It may explain that advanced technologies exist, but it should not teach unsafe DIY procedures.
3. It should clearly separate traditional maps from modern anatomical claims.
4. It should show relationships between terms instead of treating every term as a separate isolated topic.
5. It must support the 18-stage river sequence.

## Safety Rule

Knowledge visibility does not equal practice permission.

- Beginner-safe: study, reflection, simple awareness, gentle lifestyle alignment.
- Guided: long meditation, intensified pranayama, chakra contemplation, extended silence.
- Teacher-only: forced kumbhaka, bandha stacks, kundalini activation, advanced mudra, intense kriya.
